About Win95Social

What is this?

Win95Social is a fan-made, personal hobby project — a social media site styled after the look of Windows 95. It's not affiliated with, endorsed by, or made by Microsoft in any way. "Windows 95" and related trademarks belong to Microsoft Corporation; this site just borrows the visual style for fun, nostalgia, and a laugh.

Who made it?

This is an independent, non-commercial personal project. No data here is sold, shared with advertisers, or used for anything beyond running this site.

Is my data safe?

Passwords are stored securely (hashed, never in plain text). That said, this is still a small hobby project, not an enterprise platform — don't post anything here you wouldn't want public, and use a password you're not reusing somewhere important.
